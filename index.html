Blood Bank Management System

Our Mission

LifeLine is designed to optimize the availability of safe blood. We help hospitals monitor inventory levels in real-time, connect donors with those in need, and prevent the distribution of expired units — because every second counts when a life is on the line.

"Every drop of blood donated is a life saved — be the reason someone gets a second chance."

— LifeLine Blood Bank
